Who is this guy?

I am a digital strategist, creative director and user experience expert with broad-based online experience, including digital strategy, digital marketing, responsive design and development, social media outreach, email marketing, content strategy and user experience. I have been a frequent speaker on developing a meaningful digital presence for organizations and non-profits.

Until recently, I was the digital director for a global humanitarian aid organization. The digital team, which I built from the ground up, managed front and back end development, online branding, user experience, information architecture and email marketing.
